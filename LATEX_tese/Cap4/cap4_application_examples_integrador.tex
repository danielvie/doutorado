
\subsection{Double Integrator}

\subsubsection{init - configuracoes da simulacao}

The values used to simulate the double integrator example are:

- $\Omega$ represents the operation modes of the system, which are four in this case: 1, 2, 3, and 4.

- $s_{max}$ is the maximum number of operation modes, which is set to 4.

- The dynamics of the system are defined by

\begin{equation}
    \begin{matrix}
        A_1 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \\ \end{matrix} \right] &
        A_2 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \\ \end{matrix} \right] &
        A_3 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \\ \end{matrix} \right] &
        A_4 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \\ \end{matrix} \right] \\
        \\
        b_1 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right] &
        b_2 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right] &
        b_3 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ -1 \end{matrix} \right] &
        b_4 = \left[ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right]
    \end{matrix}
\end{equation}

- $t_{step}$ is the time step of the simulation, set to 0.01.

- $x_{ref}$ is the reference state vector, set to [2, -1].

- $Q$ is the weighting matrix for the trajectory computation, which is a diagonal matrix with both diagonal elements equal to 1.

- $T_{p_{max}}$ is the maximum value for the period of the trajectory cycle, set to 1.

- $T_{s}$ is the time sequence, defined as [0, 2, 3, 5, 6].

- $x_{0}$ is the initial state, set to [-0.5; -1].

\subsubsection{construindo MPC (matrizes PHI e GAMMA)}

\begin{equation}
    \Phi =
    \left[
    \begin{matrix}
        1 & 5 \\
        0 & 1
    \end{matrix}
    \right]
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
    \Gamma =
    \left[
    \begin{matrix}
        3 & 2 & 1 \\
        1 & 1 & -1
    \end{matrix}
    \right]
\end{equation}


\subsubsection{rodando simulacao}

time constraints:

\begin{equation}
    c = 
    \left[
        \begin{matrix}
            \Delta t_{r_1} -\Delta t_{r_{1, min}} \\
            \Delta t_{r_2} -\Delta t_{r_{2, min}} \\
            \vdots \\
            \Delta t_{r_{N-1}} -\Delta t_{r_{{N-1}, min}} \\
            \Delta t_{r_N} -\Delta t_{r_{N, min}} \\
        \end{matrix}
    \right] = 
    \left[
        \begin{matrix}
            - 2 + 1.625 \\
            - 1 + 0.635 \\
            - 2 + 1.625 \\
            - 1 + 0.625 \\
        \end{matrix}
    \right] =
    \left[
        \begin{matrix}
            - 0.375 \\
            - 0.365 \\
            - 0.375 \\
            - 0.365 \\
        \end{matrix}
    \right]
\end{equation}
